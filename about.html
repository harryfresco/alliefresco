UPCOMING PROJECTS

Save the Kat | status: draft 2 completed

A meta-adventure and comedy feature length film, following Kat as she finds the script of the film she is in. In a major identity crisis, Kat and her skeptical friend Annie utilise the script that she believes is keeping her prisoner. With literal plot-holes and actual 4th walls, Kat and Annie wish to confront the writer to be freed.

Bloodshed | status: draft 6 completed

A short horror comedy set in the 80’s. Six teens decide to hotbox a shed when they find a book called the dark allegiance. After reading verses from the book, a bloody and completely irrelevant coincidence occurs, killing one of the six. The teens believe someone is possessed. Chaos ensues as each point fingers at each other.

Mai Geri (Working Title) | status: draft 1 completed

A comedy drama feature film following a grieving son, Billy, after the death of family. During his struggle with grief, he teaches a karate summer camp, where he meets his new student Wally, an elderly and loveable man. Billy’s cold nature clashes with Wally’s welcoming demeanor. This is worsened when Billy’s young daughter, Keri, takes a liking to Wally.
